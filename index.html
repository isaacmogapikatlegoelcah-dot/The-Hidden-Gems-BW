About Me

Hello, I'm a First Year Student

Welcome to my personal blog! I'm currently in my first year of studies, exploring various subjects and discovering my academic interests. While I'm just beginning my journey, I'm excited to share my learning process and growth.

I created "The Hidden Gems BW" to document both my personal development as a student and my exploration of Botswana's cultural heritage. As I learn new skills through my coursework, I'm applying them to create this platform and share Botswana's unique stories.

While I don't have professional experience yet, I'm actively involved in student organizations and group projects that are helping me develop teamwork and communication skills. I'm particularly interested in how technology can be used to preserve and share cultural heritage.

When I'm not studying, you can find me exploring local cultural sites, reading about Botswana's history, or practicing the technical skills I'm learning in class. I believe that the best way to learn is by doing, which is why I've created this blog to apply my knowledge.

My goal is to use this platform to track my growth throughout my academic journey while showcasing the beautiful aspects of Botswana that often go unnoticed.
